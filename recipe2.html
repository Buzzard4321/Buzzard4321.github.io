1st mix 1 tablespoon of chocolate powder, 2 tablespoons of milk, 1 tablespoon of cream in a small glass.
Next put ice in a bowl and sprinkle lots of salt over it.
Then put the glass in the bowl.
After that build up more ice and salt around the glass.
Finally place a tea towel over it and leave to set for 1 hour stirring every few minutes
