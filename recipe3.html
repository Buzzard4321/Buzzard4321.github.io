Before we start pre heat the oven to 180 degrees
1st mix 1 cup/125ml of oats, 1/2 cup/125ml of ground almonds, 1/2 cup/125ml of plain flour in a large bowl.
Then put 1 tablespoon of coconut oil (or butter) and rub it with your finger tips until the lumps are very small
Next pour 1 tablespoon of maple syrup (or honey) in and mix well
After that put some fruit in tiny pots and if you want you can pour some cinnamon on the fruit then pour the crumble mix over the fruit
Put in the oven untill golden on top (about 25 minutes) LEAVE TO COOL BEFORE EATING
